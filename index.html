    Crime Watch

    Crime Concern

    Crime is a major worry in Malaysia, with various types of offenses posing challenges for public safety and law enforcement.

    Keeping People Safe

    Public safety is vital. It's about protecting people from harm, and it's a responsibility shared by the government, law enforcement, and the community.

    Preventing Crime

    We can prevent crime by working together. Methods like neighborhood watch, security cameras, and community support can make our communities safer.

  • Violent Crime Types

    • Murder

      Murder is intentional, unlike other killings. Planned or spontaneous.

    • Robbery

      Robbery is the unauthorised taking or attempted taking of another's property, money, or valuables by force, intimidation, or coercion.

    • Rape

      Rape is the unconsented sexual touch or penetration of another person.

    • Causing Injury

      An injury crime, often known as "assault" or "battery," involves purposefully injuring another person.
